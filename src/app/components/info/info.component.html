How to use your superpower ⚡

badge

1. By Name

Just type the name of the Pokémon you want to find! For example, type "Pikachu" or "Charizard". The Pokédex will instantly find it for you!

tag

2. By ID Number

Every Pokémon has a special number. Bulbasaur is number 1! If you know the number, just type it in and watch the magic happen.

category

3. By Elemental Type

Looking for a cool Fire-type like Charmander or a Water-type like Squirtle? Use this drop-down menu to see all Pokémon of the same type!

favorite

4. By Health Points (HP)

Want a super strong Pokémon? Type a number here to find Pokémon that have at least that much Health Points!

auto_awesome

5. Other Cool Tricks!

You can also search by the region they come from using Generation. Want to find out what powers they have? Search by their Abilities! And if you click the favorite Favorites Only button, it will only show the Pokémon you liked before!

Pro Trainer Tip

If you get stuck or want to start over, just click the Reset All button! It will clear your search so you can start fresh.

The Compare Page

Ever wonder if Pikachu is faster than Charmander? Or who has the most HP? Use the Compare Page to put up to 3 Pokémon side-by-side and see their stats face off!

add_circle
How to Add

When you are looking at a Pokémon card on the Home or Search page, look for the dark button at the bottom that says ADD TO COMPARE. Click it to send that Pokémon to the stadium!

How to Remove

Changed your mind? You can click REMOVE FROM COMPARE on their card, or go to the Compare Page and click the little X button in the corner of their picture.
